# Politics And News Forecasting

Use this skill for elections, policy decisions, court outcomes, geopolitical events, appointments, resignations, and fast-moving news.

## Evidence Sources

- official government/election/court sources
- reputable wire services
- polling aggregators
- prediction markets when rule-equivalent
- party rules and voting calendars

## Calibration Notes

- Separate "announced", "reported", "officially certified", and "resolved by rules".
- For elections, distinguish polling probability from market-implied probability and structural fundamentals.
- For legal/court questions, the exact resolution trigger often matters more than general media framing.
