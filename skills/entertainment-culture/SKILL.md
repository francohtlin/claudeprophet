# Entertainment And Culture Forecasting

Use this skill for awards, Eurovision, streaming charts, music/game releases, box office, and pop-culture rankings.

## Evidence Sources

- official award or contest rules
- nominee lists and voting windows
- platform chart methodology
- release calendars
- betting/prediction markets when available
- recent social/media momentum as weak evidence

## Calibration Notes

- Wide outcome spaces need probability mass on long-tail candidates.
- Do not collapse candidates unless the event's outcomes allow it.
- For chart questions, use historical chart volatility and current platform rankings.
